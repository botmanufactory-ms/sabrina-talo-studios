Interior Styling · Bangalow, NSW

Your space should feel like you.

I'm Sabrina — an interior stylist based in Bangalow. I work with homeowners across the Northern Rivers to create spaces that feel personal, warm, and genuinely yours. I mix new pieces with second-hand finds, build on what you already love, and shape everything around the way you actually live.

For holiday rental hosts, the brief is different — guests don't need it to feel like them, they need it to feel like somewhere worth coming back to. I style holiday properties to feel warm, considered, and inviting, so guests book again and tell their friends.

About Sabrina

A love of
real spaces.

I started Sabrina Talo Studios from a genuine love of creating spaces that feel right — not just look right. I grew up surrounded by a mix of beautiful old things and considered new ones, and that shaped how I see interiors.

Every home has a story. My job is to listen to yours, then help you tell it through the space you live in. I work with your routines, your personality, and your budget — nothing more, nothing less.
