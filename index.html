About Sabrina

A love of
real spaces.

I started Sabrina Talo Studios from a genuine love of creating spaces that feel right — not just look right. I grew up surrounded by a mix of beautiful old things and considered new ones, and that shaped how I see interiors.

Every home has a story. My job is to listen to yours, then help you tell it through the space you live in. I work with your routines, your personality, and your budget — nothing more, nothing less.
