About STS

A love of
real spaces.

I started Sabrina Talo Studios from a genuine love of creating spaces that feel right — not just look right. I grew up surrounded by a mix of beautiful old things and considered new ones, and that shaped how I see interiors.

Based in Bangalow in the Byron Bay hinterland, I work with homeowners, renters, and holiday home hosts across the region to create spaces that reflect real life — warm, personal, and full of character.

I mix curated new pieces with vintage and second-hand finds, always working within your budget. The goal is never a showroom. It's a home that feels unmistakably, beautifully yours.

How I work

The ST Approach

01

Understand

I start by listening. How do you use the space? What do you love and what frustrates you? What's your budget, your style, your life? The brief always comes from you.

02

Shape

I work with what you already have — rearranging, editing, mixing in new finds. I source from local markets, vintage stores, and trusted suppliers to find pieces that feel genuine, not purchased.

03

Deliver

The final space should make you stop in the doorway. It should feel warm, complete, and entirely yours — not styled, just right. That's the moment I work toward.
