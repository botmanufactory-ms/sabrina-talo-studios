What I Do

Your home. Your way.

Whether you own or rent, whether you're starting from scratch or just need a refresh — I work with you to create a home that makes sense for your life. No jargon. No showroom aesthetic. Just your space, thoughtfully reimagined.

I listen first. Then I look at what you have, what's working, and what's not. I mix your existing pieces with curated new additions and vintage finds to create rooms that feel complete — warm, layered, and yours.

01

Initial Chat

A relaxed conversation about your home, your life, and what you're hoping to change. No commitment required.

02

Home Walkthrough

I visit the property, assess what you have, and understand how you use each room. This is where the plan takes shape.

03

Style Plan & Sourcing

I put together a clear, costed plan — what to keep, what to move, what to add. I source pieces from local markets, vintage stores, and trusted suppliers.

04

Styling Day

I come in and style the space completely — arranging, layering, and finishing every room until it feels right. You come home to something new.
