What I Do

A space guests can't wait to return to.

Holiday homes live or die by their first impression — the listing photo, the arrival, the moment a guest walks in and decides whether this feels special. I style properties to create that feeling: warm, considered, genuinely inviting.

I work within your budget, mixing curated new pieces with vintage and second-hand finds. The goal is a home that feels authentic — not like every other rental on the platform.

01

Consultation

We walk through the property together (or virtually) and I assess what you have, what you need, and what would make the biggest impact within your budget.

02

Style Plan

I put together a clear plan: what stays, what goes, what to source. No surprises on cost. Everything tied to your brief and your property's character.

03

Styling Day

I come in, arrange, layer, and style every room — including accessorising for the photographer. The result is a space ready to shoot and list.

04

Final Styling Guide

I leave you with a simple reset guide so you (or your cleaners) can maintain the look between guests.

Better Photos

Styled spaces photograph dramatically better. Better photos = more clicks, more bookings.

Higher Ratings

Guests notice when a space is thoughtfully put together. Expect more 5-star reviews on presentation.

Budget-Friendly

Long-Lasting
